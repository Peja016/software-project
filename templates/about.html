About Dublin Bike Share

Dublin Bike Share is the capital's premier bicycle sharing system, designed to provide an affordable, eco-friendly, and convenient transportation alternative for residents and visitors alike. Since our launch, we've transformed how people move around Dublin, reducing traffic congestion and carbon emissions while promoting healthier lifestyles.

Our Mission

Dublin Bike Share is committed to providing sustainable, accessible transportation options that enhance urban mobility while reducing environmental impact. We aim to:

🌱

Environmental Sustainability

Reduce carbon emissions and promote eco-friendly transportation alternatives to combat climate change and improve Dublin's air quality.

🚲

Accessible Mobility

Make cycling accessible to everyone by providing affordable, convenient transportation options throughout the city.

🏙️

Urban Development

Support Dublin's growth as a smart, sustainable city by integrating cycling infrastructure with other public transportation systems.

Our History

2008

Planning Phase

Dublin City Council initiated plans for a bike-sharing program after analyzing successful models in other European cities. Partnerships were formed with key stakeholders to develop the infrastructure and operational framework.

2009

Official Launch

Dublin Bike Share was officially launched with 450 bicycles and 40 stations strategically placed throughout the city center. The program received overwhelming public support, with over 1,000 subscriptions on the first day.

2013

Expansion Phase 1

Following tremendous success, the service expanded to include 950 bicycles and 65 stations, covering a wider area of Dublin and serving more communities.

2018

Expansion Phase 2

The network grew to include over 1,600 bicycles and 115 stations, along with the introduction of a modernized mobile app for improved user experience.

2023

Technological Upgrades

Introduction of smart bikes with GPS tracking, improved station accessibility, and enhanced digital interfaces for better user experience.

2025

Looking Forward

Dublin Bike Share continues to evolve with plans for further expansion, integration with other transport systems, and adoption of the latest sustainable technologies.
