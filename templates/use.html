Safety First

At Dublin Bike, safety is our top priority. It influences the way we design and enhance our bikes, shape rider education and training, and collaborate with cities to redesign streets, ensuring that people come before cars.

Dublin Bike Safety Policies

  1. Please comply with the traffic safety laws and regulations of the area while riding. Dublin Bike is not responsible for any safety accidents caused by non-compliance with traffic laws and regulations, including but not limited to riding against traffic, ignoring traffic signals, dangerous riding, etc.
  2. To independently use Dublin Bike, you must be at least 18 years old. Young individuals between the ages of 14 and 18 should use Dublin Bike under the supervision of a guardian. Children under the age of 14 are not permitted to use Dublin Bike.
  3. We strongly recommend that you wear a helmet while riding, and we also welcome you to use your own helmet.
  4. We advise you to purchase accident insurance for transportation vehicles before riding.
