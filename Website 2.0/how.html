How to Use Dublin Bike Share

Using Dublin Bike Share is quick and easy. Our bike-sharing service provides a convenient and eco-friendly way to navigate Dublin's streets. Follow the simple steps below to start your journey.

1

Register for an Account

Sign up through our website or mobile app. You'll need to provide some basic information and payment details. We offer various subscription options including annual memberships, 3-day passes, and pay-as-you-go plans.

2

Find a Bike Station

Use our map to locate the nearest bike station. Dublin Bike Share has over 115 stations strategically placed throughout the city center and surrounding areas. Each station is equipped with multiple bikes ready for use.

3

Unlock a Bike

At the station, you can unlock a bike using our mobile app or by inserting your card into the station terminal. Follow the on-screen instructions, and the system will release a bike for you. Check that the bike is in good condition before riding.

4

Enjoy Your Ride

Cycle safely and follow all traffic rules. Dublin Bike Share bikes are designed for urban riding with adjustable seats and easy handling. The first 30 minutes of each journey are typically free for subscribers (depending on your plan).

5

Return the Bike

When you've reached your destination, return the bike to any Dublin Bike Share station. Simply push the bike firmly into an available dock until you hear a beep and see the green light, indicating a secure lock. Make sure to check that the bike is properly docked to avoid any extra charges.
